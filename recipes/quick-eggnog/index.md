# Quick Eggnog

- Total time: 15 minutes
    - Prep time: 15 minutes
- Yield: 1 (small) serving

## Inventory

### Ingredients


- 1 egg
- 2 tsp granulated sugar
- ⅛ teaspoon vanilla extract
- Pinch ground nutmeg
- Piiinch of salt
- ⅓ - ½ cup milk


### Special Equipment

- Hand mixer
- Strainer

## Instructions

### Prepare

1. In small bowl
    1. Mix on high until nearly overmixed
        - **1 egg**
        - **2 tsp granulated sugar**
        - **⅛ teaspoon vanilla extract**
        - **Pinch ground nutmeg**
        - **Piiinch of salt**
    1. Mix until desired taste
        - **⅓ - ½ cup milk**
1. Strain into glass
1. Top with **nutmeg**
