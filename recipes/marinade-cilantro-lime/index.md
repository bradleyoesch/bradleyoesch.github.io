# Cilantro Lime Marinade

- Total time: 5 minutes
- Yield: for ~1 lbs meat

## Inventory

### Ingredients

_Per pound of meat_

- 4 T **extra-virgin olive oil**
- 2 T **cilantro**, chopped
- 2 T **lime juice**
- 1 cloves **garlic**, minced
- ½ tsp **crushed red pepper flakes**
- ½ tsp **cumin**
- ½ tsp **paprika**
- 1 tsp **kosher salt**
- ¼ tsp **freshly ground black pepper**
