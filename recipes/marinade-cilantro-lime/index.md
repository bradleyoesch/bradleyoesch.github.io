# Cilantro Lime Marinade

- Total time: 5 minutes
- Yield: for ~1 lbs meat

## Inventory

### Ingredients

_Per pound of meat_

- 4 T olive oil
- 2 T fresh cilantro, chopped
- 2 T lime juice
- 1 cloves garlic, minced
- ½ tsp crushed red pepper flakes
- ½ tsp cumin
- ½ tsp paprika
- 1 tsp salt
- ¼ tsp black pepper
