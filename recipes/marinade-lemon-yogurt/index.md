# Lemon Yogurt Marinade

- Total time: 5 minutes
- Yield: for ~1-2 lbs meat

## Inventory

### Ingredients

_Per pound of meat_

- ⅓ cup plain greek yogurt
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 1 lemon, zested, juiced
- 4-5 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 T dried oregano
- 1 tsp kosher salt
- ½ tsp freshly ground black pepper

### Special equipment

- Microplane/zester
- Hand juicer

## Notes

- Save about half for basting or sauce
