# Fajita Marinade

- Total time: 5 minutes
- Yield: for ~1 lbs meat

## Inventory

### Ingredients

_Per pound of meat_

- 2 T **extra-virgin olive oil**
- 1½ T **lime juice** (about 1-2 limes)
- 4 T **cilantro**, chopped
- 2 cloves **garlic**, minced
- 1 tsp **chili powder**
- ½ tsp **cumin**
- ½ tsp **kosher salt**
- ¼ tsp **dried oregano**
- ¼ tsp **onion powder**
- ¼ tsp **freshly ground black pepper**
